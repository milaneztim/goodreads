A net of shadows begins to tighten around the Shadowhunters of the London Institute. Mortmain plans to use his Infernal Devices, an army of pitiless automatons, to destroy the Shadowhunters. He needs only one last item to complete his plan: he needs Tessa Gray.

Ending a trilogy in the right way is no easy task and this is the second trilogy I finish by Cassie and she knows how to deliver an ending!! The writing is great, I find it has many many great quotes through the book. I don’t believe CC is “stealing” other authors work because she obviously can write and she has her own voice! I was frustrated before regarding the excessive humor in the last book and I think it was tamed down here and much more appropriate.


The characters were excellent, I am also always saying that the key to a huge success in the writing world is memorable characters which Cassie surely had in this trilogy. All the characters were fleshed out, unique and I loved the romance! I think Cassie just knows how to create the perfect ships! Speaking of ships here, this has the best love trilogy of all the books I read! And all characters got the development they deserved here.


Clockwork prince had many filler moments without much progress to the plot which was not the case here and we got all the answers we wanted!
