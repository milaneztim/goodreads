This earthly plane offers much for us to learn: happiness, wisdom, loss, heartbreak, and enlightenment. It is a Pandoras box of emotions, situations, opportunities, and failures, all wrapped into a package we call life. Nobody is immune, but everyone has the opportunity to grow tall or wither like a flower in harsh light. Its completely up to us how we choose to respond. Finding Hope in the Darkness of Grief is a gleaning of insights from artist Diamante Lavender. For her, life has been a long, difficult road, but it has taught many poignant lessons. Her poetry collection is an exploration of the human soul, a traversing of situations that life throws at us. Diamante has always been intrigued by the ability to overcome and move on to bigger and better things. She writes to encourage hope and possibility in those who read her stories. If she can help others heal, as she has, then Diamantes work as an author and artist will have been well spent. From illusions of darkness and separation to the realities of harboring false beliefs and following the road to healing, Lavendar's verse and insights follow the process of not only recovery; but finding the kind of empathy and understanding from the healing process that strengthens other connections in life.

The juxtaposition of nature-oriented images and art with these admonitions lends a visual touch to the written word that enhances both with a structure and reinforcement that either alone could not have achieved.

Those who will benefit most from Lavendar's approach are readers who are seeking their own paths to healing and greater enlightenment, who can accept her candid assessments of what the process of grieving leads to ("Death is not an ending but a beginning to a different reality, not only/for the bereaved but also for those who have passed into eternity.").

Finding Hope in the Darkness of Grief, by Diamante Lavendar (author of Breaking the Silence and Poetry and Ponderings: A Journey of Abuse and Healing Through Poetry). Published by Balboa Press, 1st edition, 2018. 74 pages (print edition). Genre: Inspirational Poetry / Religious Poetry / Supernaturalism / Overcoming grief.

With the guiding principle that loss is an illusion and that grief can be overcome through a positive attitude and the exercise of an active, intentional, dedicated, self-controlled and grateful perseverance, the artist and writer Diamante Lavendar performs, in Finding Hope in The Darkness of Grief, a poetic exercise in overcoming grief, based on two personal intuitions: that loss is opposition and therefore offers an opportunity, and that there is a superior spirit and a divine destiny. Sharing her personal experience and proposing a positive approach to grief, the author proposes a kind of poetic and spiritual guidance for those willing to work actively in overcoming their own griefs.

The inspiring and positive journey of reading this book is rather short (74 pages). The style of the writing has seemed to me like a gentle swing: at times it is very beautiful, allegorical and profound, while in other sections the ideas are more direct and basic sentences. I have found in this book small capsules of true wisdom, bathed in a mystical halo that starts from the belief in a Spirit (God), in heaven and in the idea that we humans are infinite beings that are on earth only being "Trained for a greater purpose". Therefore, I would recommend Finding Hope in the Darkness of Grief to those readers whose spirituality, sensitivity and character are in an open state that allows them to translate their own duels into positive thoughts, understanding that the positive state is not a matter of luck, but rather a personal decision based on surrender and understanding.

Finding Hope in the Darkness of Grief, por Diamante Lavendar (autora de Breaking the Silence and Poetry y Ponderings: A Journey of Abuse and Healing Through Poetry). Publicada por Balboa Press, 1ª. edición, 2018. 74 páginas (edición impresa). Género: Poesía Inspiradora / Poesía Religiosa / Supernaturalismo / Superación del duelo.

Con el eje rector de que la pérdida es una ilusión y de que el duelo puede ser superado mediante una actitud positiva y el ejercicio de una perseverancia activa, intencional, dedicada, autocontrolada y agradecida, la artista y escritora Diamante Lavendar realiza en Finding Hope in the Darkness of Grief un ejercicio poético de superación del duelo a partir de dos intuiciones personales: que la pérdida es oposición y por lo tanto ofrece una oportunidad, y que existe un espíritu superior y un destino divino. Compartiendo su experiencia personal y proponiendo un enfoque positivo alrededor del duelo, la autora plantea una especie de guía poética y espiritual para aquellos dispuestos a trabajar activamente en la superación de sus propios duelos.

El viaje inspirador y positivo que supone la lectura de este libro es más bien corto (74 páginas). El estilo de la escritura me ha parecido un suave vaivén: por momentos es muy bello, alegórico y profundo, mientras que en otras secciones las ideas son sentencias más directas y básicas. He encontrado en este libro pequeñas cápsulas de verdadera sabiduría, bañadas en un halo místico que parte de la creencia en un Espíritu (Dios), en un cielo metafísico y en la idea de que los humanos somos seres infinitos que estamos en la tierra únicamente siendo “entrenados para un propósito mayor”.
